For Teachers

Our team of educators are passionate about making learning engaging and fun. Whether you're teaching science, chemistry, history, or art, we can tailor our tours and workshops to meet your specific curriculum requirements. And we are always open to new ideas and suggestions from teachers.
We know that the best learning experiences happen when students are engaged and excited about what they are learning. We use the latest technology and multimedia resources to bring our exhibits to life. From interactive displays to virtual reality simulations, we strive to make our museum a place where students can explore, experiment, and discover in a hands-on, immersive environment.
Our museum is committed to promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion in education. We believe that every student deserves access to quality education, regardless of their background or circumstances. We work with teachers and schools from all over the community to ensure that our programs and resources are accessible and inclusive to all.
